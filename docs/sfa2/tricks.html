Tips and Tricks

Select Winning Pose

When you knock the other guy out, press Start and one of the attack buttons to pick which one you want.

Stage Select

1. Highlight the fighter whose stage you want to fight on.

2. Press and hold Start for about 2 seconds and 5 seconds for hidden stages (M. Bison, Sagat, etc.).

3. With Start still held down, choose the guy you want to use.

Use Chun Li in her Super Turbo outfit

1. Press and hold Start

2. Go to Chun Li for 5 seconds.

3. Press a button.

4. Let go of Start.

Play as Evil Ryu (Move list below)

1. Go to Ryu

2. Press Start

3. Go to Adon, Akuma, Adon, Ryu

4. Press and hold Start and then press P or K

Note: This trick is for the Sega Saturn ONLY. It is not on PlayStation or SNES.

Evil Ryu Moves

Fireball - D, DF, F+P

Hurricane Kick - D, DB, B+K (also can be done in air)

Dragon Punch - F, D, DF+P

Ashura Warp - B, D, DB or F, D, DF+all 3 Kicks or all 3 Punches

Fake Fireball - D, DF, F+Start

Evil Ryu Super Combos

Super Fireball - D, DF, F, D, DF, F+P

Super Hurricane Kick - D, DB, B, D, DB, B+K

Messatsu Uppercut - D, DF, F, D, DF+K

Raging Demon - Jab, Jab, F, Short, Fierce (Level 3 only)

Play as Champion Edition Dhalsim

1. Go to Dhalsim

2. Press Start

3. Go to Zangief, Sagat, Charlie, Dhalsim

4. Press and hold Start and then press P or K

Play as Champion Edition Zangief

1. Go to Zangief

2. Press Start

3. Go to Sagat, Sodom, Rose, Birdie, Charlie, Dhalsim, Ryu, Adon, Chun Li, Guy, Ken, and Zangief

4. Press and hold Start and then press P or K

Fight against Super Akuma

1. On the 1 player side use the 1 Player color, on the 2 player side use the 2 player color.

2. Get to the end boss without losing a round

3. Get 3 Perfects

4. Get 5 Super Finishes or Custom Finishes.
