Street Fighter Zero 2 - Cheats

Select a Different Costume

After selecting your character press and hold Start + Low Punch or Low Kick until the fight begins.

Selecting Gouki (Akuma)

  1. Move the cursor to Ryu and hold Start for one second.
  2. Then move the cursor to Adon and hold Start for one second.
  3. Then move the cursor to Sakura and hold Start for one second.
  4. Then move the cursor to Rolento and hold Start for one second.
  5. Finally, back to Rose and hold Start for one second before you press any button.

Selecting Shin Gouki (True Akuma)

  1. Move the cursor to Gouki while holding Start.
  2. Move up and you will select the True Akuma.

Selecting Original Chun-Li

  1. Move the cursor to Chun-Li while holding Start.
  2. Move down and you will select the original Chun-Li.

Selecting Original Zangief

  1. Move the cursor to Zangief while holding Start.
  2. Move down and you will select the original Zangief.

Selecting Original Dhalsim

  1. Move the cursor to Dhalsim while holding Start.
  2. Move down and you will select the original Dhalsim.

Selecting M. Bison (Vega)

  1. Move the cursor to Vega while holding Start.
  2. Move up and you will select the original M. Bison.
